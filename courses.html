Courses

Here's what we have to offer

J'elevates is a unique program that uses a systemic phonics approach to teach word-formation. Using the phonic system enables fluency in reading and writing. At J'elevates, we employ fun, multisensory teaching processes and age-appropriate materials for teaching reading and writing.  Our learning program is designed to enhance reading, writing and comprehension for all ages.
Course Duration:

There are three reading levels in the Basic programme: the red, yellow and green learning levels. Every level takes about three months to complete. The duration may vary depending on the pace of each student. There are 12 sessions in a month. A session lasts for 60-90 minutes. 

Classes:

Classes are structured to accommodate a minimum of 5 students to a maximum of 10 students. The course is designed to provide rotational check in sessions with each student. Currently classes are hosted virtually. Age: Students from four years and above.

 A placement assessment will be used to determine students learning level.
