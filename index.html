J'Elevates

laying the building blocks for education

Course info

J'Elevates is a unique program that uses a systemic phonic approach to teach word formation. Using the phonic system enables fluency in reading and writing.

Our learning program is designed to enhance reading, writing and comprehension for all ages. We employ fun, multisensory teaching processes and age-appropriate materials to teach reading and writing.

We believe just like Dr. Martin Kozloff rightly said, If a child memorizes ten words, the child can read ten words but if a child learns ten letter sounds, they are able to read:
  • 350 three-sound words
  • 4320 four-sound words
  • 21,650 five-sound words
